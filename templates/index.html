Diabetes is a chronic, long-lasting health condition
that affects how your body turns food into energy



Symptoms

Lose weight without trying
Have blurry vision
Have numb or tingling hands or feet
Have very dry skin
Urinate (pee) a lot
Have sores that heal slowly

Caused By

The exact cause of most types of diabetes is unknown
Genetic or environmental factors
Gestational diabetes are more common in people who are overweight or obese.

Remedies

Eat healthy foods.
Get more physical activity.
Lose excess pounds
Diabetes medication or insulin therapy. Blood sugar monitoring.
